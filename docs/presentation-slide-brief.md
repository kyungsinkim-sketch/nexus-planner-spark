# Slide Design Brief — Re-Be.io Presentation
## Thailand Ad Festival, March 2026

---

## Overall Design Direction

**Style**: Apple Keynote / Vercel-inspired minimal
**Background**: Pure black (#000000) throughout
**Typography**: White (#FFFFFF) primary, gray (#888888) secondary. Sans-serif, clean (SF Pro, Inter, or Helvetica Neue)
**Accent color**: Gold (#D4A843) — used sparingly for key highlights only
**Layout principle**: One idea per slide. Let text breathe. No clutter.
**No**: bullet points, stock photos, gradients, corporate templates, logos on every slide
**Yes**: bold single statements, strategic whitespace, cinematic feel

---

## Slide-by-Slide Specification

### SLIDE 1 — Opening Hook
- Black screen
- Center-aligned, large white text:
  > "Intelligence is not about what you know.
  > It is about how successfully you interact with the world."
- No logo. No title. Just the quote.
- Feel: cinematic, like a movie opening

### SLIDE 2 — Personal Story (no slide or minimal)
- Option A: Black screen only (speaker talks without visual distraction)
- Option B: Single line in gray, small text at bottom: "15 years of pitches, failures, and bad restaurants"
- This section is carried entirely by the speaker's voice

### SLIDE 3a — "Where does experience live?"
- Center text, large: **"Where does experience live?"**
- Below in smaller gray text: "Slack · WhatsApp · Email · Notion · Meetings"
- Feel: the tools listed feel scattered, fragmented

### SLIDE 3b — Three Scenarios
- Three cards or blocks, horizontally arranged:
  - **01** "Your best producer leaves" — icon: door/exit
  - **02** "Same client, same delay, third time" — icon: loop/repeat
  - **03** "How do you transfer judgment?" — icon: brain/person
- Dark cards with thin white borders, minimal icons
- Each scenario appears one at a time (animation: fade in left to right)

### SLIDE 3c — "This is a memory problem"
- Black screen
- Large bold white text, center: **"This is not a knowledge problem."**
- Beat, then second line appears: **"This is a memory problem."**
- High impact moment — keep it clean

### SLIDE 4a — Three AI Layers
- Vertical stack or timeline, bottom to top:
  - Layer 1 (gray/dim): **Knowledge AI** — Documents → Retrieval
  - Layer 2 (gray/dim): **Automation AI** — Operational Data → Execution
  - Layer 3 (gold accent, bright): **Re-Be.io** — Decisions & Collaboration → Agent Evolution
- The first two layers should feel "past/established"
- Re-Be layer should feel elevated, highlighted with gold accent
- Animation: layers appear bottom to top, Re-Be layer glows or highlights last

### SLIDE 4b — "The Missing Layer"
- Center text: **"The Missing Layer"**
- Below, five words in a row with generous spacing:
  > Decisions · Trade-offs · Alignment · Negotiation · Judgment
- Below that, in smaller gray text:
  > "None of this is structured. None of this is owned. None of this becomes memory."

### SLIDE 5a — "Collaborative Experience Layer"
- Re-Be.io logo (small, top left)
- Large center text: **"The Collaborative Experience Layer"**
- Subtitle in gray: "Not just what your team did — but why they decided to do it."

### SLIDE 5b — "Record"
- Left-aligned large text: **"Record"**
- Right side: simplified UI mockup or diagram showing:
  - Meeting → AI extracts → Decision card (who, what, why, alternatives)
- Caption: "Conversation becomes structured memory."
- Keep the mockup abstract/minimal — not a full screenshot

### SLIDE 5c — "Reflect"
- Left-aligned large text: **"Reflect"**
- Right side: pattern visualization — e.g., timeline with repeating markers
- Example quotes in speech bubbles or cards:
  > "Client revisions consistently delay launch by 2 weeks."
  > "Budget cuts always follow late-stage direction changes."
- Caption: "AI connects dots across time."

### SLIDE 5d — "Realize Better"
- Left-aligned large text: **"Realize Better"**
- Right side: agent suggestion UI mockup — chat-style bubble:
  > "This pattern appeared in your last 3 projects. Add buffer?"
- Caption: "From reflection to anticipation."

### SLIDE 5e — "Record → Reflect → Realize Better"
- Full-width horizontal flow:
  **Record** → **Reflect** → **Realize Better**
- Three icons or abstract shapes connected by arrows
- Gold accent on "Realize Better"
- This is the summary slide before the video

### SLIDE 6 — Video
- Black screen with centered play button
- Or: "Let me show you what this looks like in practice." in small gray text
- Video plays full screen

### SLIDE 7a — "Who owns this intelligence?"
- Black screen
- Large center text: **"Who owns this intelligence?"**
- Dramatic pause slide

### SLIDE 7b — Two Types of Intelligence
- Split screen or two cards:
  - Left card (gray): **Organizational Context** — "Project details, client info, team structures → Stays with the company"
  - Right card (gold border): **Personal Experience** — "Decision patterns, learning, instincts, growth → Belongs to you"
- Visual emphasis on the right card (gold accent, slightly larger)

### SLIDE 7c — "Experience Sovereignty"
- Center text, bold: **"Experience Sovereignty"**
- Below in gray: "Your accumulated wisdom follows you. Across companies. Across your career."
- Optional: abstract visual of a person carrying a glowing orb/data shape (representing their experience)

### SLIDE 8 — From Collaboration to Recognition
- Horizontal flow:
  **Collaboration** → **Experience** → **Reputation**
- Below: three concepts
  - "Verified contributions"
  - "Validated decisions"
  - "Proven judgment"
- Feel: forward-looking, aspirational

### SLIDE 9 — Closing
- Black screen
- Two lines, appearing sequentially:
  > "The best creative intelligence isn't artificial."
  > "It's accumulated."
- Final beat: **"Thank you."** in smaller text
- Optional: Re-Be.io logo + website URL at very bottom, subtle

## Animation Notes
- All transitions: simple fade or cut. No flashy transitions.
- Text reveals: fade in, or appear line by line for dramatic moments
- Slides 3c, 7a, 9: hold on black for 2-3 seconds before text appears (build tension)
- Three-layer diagram (4a): animate bottom to top
- Record/Reflect/Realize (5b-5d): consistent left-text, right-visual layout

## Technical Notes
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Export as both Keynote and PDF
- Video slot: leave placeholder, embed separately
- Font fallback: if custom font unavailable, use system sans-serif
- All text must be readable from the back of a large conference room (min 28pt for body, 48pt+ for headlines)

---

## Tone Summary
Think: **TED Talk meets Apple Keynote meets Cannes Lions stage.**
Not a product demo. Not a startup pitch. A *vision talk* with a product behind it.
The slides should feel like they belong in a film — not a boardroom.
